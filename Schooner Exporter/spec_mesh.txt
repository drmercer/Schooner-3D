short: 0x0001 (version short)
byte: flags {textured, armature-indexed}
short: i (number of tris)
short: v (number of vertices)

short[i *3]: indices

float[v *3]: vertex positions

short: d (number of double pairs)
short[d*2]: doubles (indices of vertices that are the same geometrically)

if textured
	float[v *2]: uv coordinates
	
if armature-indexed
	for each vertex (v)
		byte: b (number of bones with nonzero weights)
		byte[b]: indices of bones with nonzero weights
		float[b]: weight for each bone
